Project Background

In February 2024, I collaborated with Human Succour, a caregiving NGO based in Pittsburgh, USA, to create a promotional poster. Human Succour is dedicated to providing compassionate care and support for individuals in need, focusing on enhancing their well-being and health.

Project Brief

The objective was to design a poster that effectively communicates the services offered by Human Succour while resonating with their target audience. The design needed to convey a sense of care, professionalism, and warmth, encouraging potential clients to reach out for assistance.

Goals
  • Create a visually appealing poster that highlights the key services of Human Succour.
  • Use engaging visuals and typography to enhance clarity and appeal.
  • Ensure the contact information and service details are prominently displayed.
Process
  • Research and Concept Development: I explored effective designs used in the healthcare and caregiving sectors, focusing on elements that evoke trust and compassion. This informed the design direction to align with Human Succour’s mission.
  • Content Integration: I incorporated the essential details, including service offerings, contact information, and location, ensuring they were clear and easy to read.
  • Visual Design: I selected a soothing color palette and engaging imagery that reflects the caring nature of the services provided. The typography was chosen to enhance readability while conveying a friendly tone.
  • Feedback and Iteration: I shared the initial design with the Human Succour team for feedback, making necessary adjustments to ensure the final product met their expectations and effectively conveyed their message.
Unique Solution

The final poster features a calming and inviting design that captures the essence of Human Succour’s caregiving services. The combination of friendly imagery and clear typography emphasizes their commitment to client well-being. The layout ensures that essential information is easily accessible, encouraging potential clients to reach out for assistance.

Tools Used
  • Adobe Illustrator: For creating graphic elements and layout.
  • Adobe Photoshop: For image editing and enhancements.
Experience Gained

This project allowed me to contribute to a meaningful cause, utilizing design to promote the vital services offered by Human Succour. I hope the poster effectively resonates with the community and encourages individuals to seek the care they need.
