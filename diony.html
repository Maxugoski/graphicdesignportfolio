Project Background

In 2021, my high school friend Victor shared his vision of starting a record label, Luciano Records. After years of music training and securing investors, he was ready to bring his dream to life. Victor's passion for music and desire to create a legacy for future generations inspired me to take on this project, aiming to encapsulate his vision in a timeless brand.

Project Brief

Victor wanted a logo and branding that reflected the essence of Luciano Records. The design needed to resonate with both artists and audiences, embodying quality and legacy. The challenge was to create something distinctive yet versatile enough to adapt across various media, from album covers to merchandise.

Goals
  • Develop a logo that represents the brand's commitment to quality music
  • Ensure the branding is timeless and suitable for future generations.
  • Create a cohesive visual identity that can be easily recognized and remembered.
Process
  • Research and Inspiration: I explored various music industry logos, focusing on timeless designs that convey sophistication and creativity.
  • Concept Development: I sketched multiple concepts, incorporating elements that symbolize music and legacy, such as musical notes and classic typography.
  • Feedback Loop: I presented initial designs to Victor, gathering his insights and refining the concepts based on his preferences.
  • Final Design: After several iterations, I created a polished logo that encapsulated Victor's vision.
Unique Solution

The final design features a sleek, modern logo combined with a classic font. The use of negative space within the logo subtly hints at musical elements, creating a unique visual identity that stands out while reflecting the brand's essence. This balance of modernity and tradition aligns perfectly with Victor's goal of building a lasting legacy.

Tools Used
  • Adobe Illustrator: For vector design and logo creation.
  • Adobe Photoshop: For mockups and visual presentations.
  • Procreate: For initial sketches and brainstorming ideas.
